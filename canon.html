But when gunpowder came along, later Earl owners added gun holes to fire from and filled passages in the landward curtain wall with masonry. One of these gun holes was in the lower hall block, pointing out towards the sea to help defend the castle against ships. Gunners were rewarded in 1548 for firing on English Ships during an encounter on the Firth of the Forth. You can now choose to continue towards the cliff-side, or head back to the hallway entrance.
